             │ HTTP/WebSocket
             ↓
┌─────────────────────────┐
│  Express.js Backend     │
│  (REST API + Socket.io) │
└────────────┬────────────┘
             │
             ↓
┌─────────────────────────┐

## Presentation Tips for Delivery

### 🎤 Speaking Tips:

1. **Start with Problem** - Engage emotionally ("Every business owner knows this frustration...")
2. **Use Stories** - Give real examples, not just features
3. **Show Enthusiasm** - Believe in what you're building
4. **Make Eye Contact** - Connect with judges/audience
5. **Speak Clearly** - Pace at 120-150 words/minute
6. **Use Pauses** - Let important points sink in
7. **Handle Questions** - Listen fully, answer concisely

### 🎨 Visual Design Tips:

1. **Keep Slides Simple** - Max 5 bullet points per slide
2. **Use Visuals** - Charts, mockups, screenshots better than text
3. **Consistent Branding** - Same fonts, colors throughout
4. **High Contrast** - Easy to read from distance
5. **Thank You Slide** - End professionally with contact info

### ⏱️ Time Management:

- Total Time: ~5-10 minutes
- Problem: 30 sec
- Solution: 1 min
- Features: 1 min
- Demo: 2 min
- Impact: 30 sec
- Future: 30 sec
- Questions: 1 min

### 💡 Demo Best Practices:

1. **Have Backup Plan** - Screenshots if demo fails
2. **Pre-load Data** - Don't spend time entering data
3. **Highlight Key Features** - Don't demo everything
4. **Tell a Story** - "Here's what a user would see..."
5. **Practice Timing** - Demo should be max 2 min

### 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid:

❌ Reading slides word-by-word
❌ Going over time limit
❌ Too much technical jargon
❌ Not getting to the point quickly
❌ Forgetting to mention the problem
❌ Weak call to action
❌ Poor eye contact
❌ Speaking too fast
